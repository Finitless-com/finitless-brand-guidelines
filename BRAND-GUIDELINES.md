## 1. Logo

### Primary Logo
> Place primary logo files in `logos/primary/`

**Minimum size**: _TBD_
**Clear space**: _TBD_ (minimum padding around the logo)

### Secondary Versions
> Place in `logos/secondary/`

- **White version** - for dark backgrounds
- **Dark version** - for light backgrounds
- **Monochrome** - for single-color contexts

### Icon / Favicon
> Place in `logos/icon/`

Sizes needed:
- 16x16 (favicon)
- 32x32 (favicon)
- 180x180 (Apple touch icon)
- 192x192 (Android)
- 512x512 (PWA)

### Wordmark
> Place in `logos/wordmark/`

Text-only version of the logo for contexts where the icon isn't suitable.

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not change colors outside the approved palette
- Do not place on busy backgrounds without contrast
- Do not add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
