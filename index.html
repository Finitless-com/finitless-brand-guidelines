Voice & Tone

How we communicate across every touchpoint.

Personality

Confident, not arrogant

We state facts and let results speak.

✓ "Finitless captures orders your staff would miss."
✗ "We are the best AI company in the restaurant industry."

Revolutionary, not combative

We empower restaurants. We don't attack platforms.

✓ "The future of restaurant ordering is already here."
✗ "Traditional ordering systems are dead."

Practical, not visionary

Dollars saved today, not a distant future.

✓ "Recover $31,000 in missed orders this year."
✗ "Imagine a world where every restaurant uses AI."

Direct, not salesy

Straight talk about ROI and results.

✓ "You miss 40% of calls. That is $31,000 walking out the door."
✗ "Do not miss this incredible limited-time opportunity!"
